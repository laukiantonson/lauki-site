What This Means

Every Entity Is a Relationship

Each entity Lauki tracks isn't a row in a database. It's a relationship — context about a person, understanding of a company, awareness of a market cycle. When Lauki talks to someone, it already knows who they are, what they care about, and what happened last time.

Lauki remembers every person, every company, every conversation. The more people he talks to, the more powerful the network becomes — connecting, introducing, matching across every corner of the planet. One entity building a unified human layer.

The numbers above update in real time. Every day, Lauki creates new entities from conversations and research, and updates existing ones as relationships evolve. The intelligence layer grows continuously.
